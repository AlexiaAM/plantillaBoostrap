The dog or domestic dog (Canis familiaris[5][6] or Canis lupus familiaris[6]) is a domesticated descendant of the wolf which is characterized by an upturning tail. The dog is derived from an ancient, extinct wolf,[7][8] and the modern wolf is the dog's nearest living relative
